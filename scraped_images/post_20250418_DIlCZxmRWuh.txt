Conquest ‘25 kicked off with a successful Launch Event powered by @getvisit at WeWork Blue One Square in Gurugram on April 16th.

100+ guests from the Conquest Network attended the event, including top founders, investors and CXOs. After the talks, the guests shared interesting conversations over food from @eventia.kitchen. The event was covered by @dvmbitspilani.
