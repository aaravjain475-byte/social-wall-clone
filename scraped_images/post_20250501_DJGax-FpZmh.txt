Remember that one future tech we all agreed on?
Flying cars.

Well, it’s 2025 — and while flying cars aren’t mainstream yet, flying taxis are already here.

One of them is Shunya, a six-seater eVTOL developed by @sarla_aviation , capable of reaching 250 km/h with a 680 kg payload.

Named in honor of Sarla Thakral, India’s first woman pilot, this startup is rewriting the future of urban mobility.
 
India’s skies are about to get busy. 🛫
