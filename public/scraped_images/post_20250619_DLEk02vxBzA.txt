Did you know over 63 million Indians face significant hearing loss, yet most workplaces and public spaces still aren’t accessible for them?

@glovatrix, founded by @aishwaryakarnataki and Parikshit Sohoni, is on a mission to change that with their AI-powered gesture recognition gloves, enabling real-time, two-way communication for deaf people globally.
